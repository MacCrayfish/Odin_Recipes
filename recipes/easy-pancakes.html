Easy Pancakes

Description

These are some fast and simple pancakes for nourishment. Enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 30g Melted Butter
  • 200g Plain flour
  • Sugar to taste
  • 2 Eggs
  • 100ml Milk

How to make

  1. Melt the butter for 10 seconds in the microwave.
  2. Mix the flour and the sugar together in a bowl.
  3. Mix in the butter, eggs and milk.
  4. Whisk thoroughly.
  5. Pour into a preheated circular pan on high heat.
  6. Flip after roughly 30 seconds.
  7. Add preferred toppings.
  8. Consume to restore your strength.
  9. Become as powerful as you once were.

Editors note

The pan used does not need to be round.
